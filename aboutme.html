Hi! I'm Shree Khangaonkar!

I'm an aspiring Electrical Engineer with a passion for music and audio technology. I'm currently studying Electrical and Computer Engineering/Physics at the University of Southern California.

My projects live at the intersection of engineering and creativity: from Experience Synesthesia, a sensory-blending art and engineering installation that ties sound, light, and touch together, to designing and building custom high-fidelity speakers from scratch, including enclosure modeling and analog crossover design. I’ve experimented with generative models through an unconditional audio generator trained on spectrograms, and developed Investigate the Animals, a Where’s Waldo-style Java game that combines playful interaction with thoughtful UI and object-oriented design. Alongside that, I’ve designed and managed custom websites for clients, creating responsive, visually clean interfaces that serve real people and real needs.

Right now, I’m especially interested in how physical systems, acoustics, and interactive media come together. Whether that’s tuning a speaker, wiring a circuit, or building tools that reshape how people experience sound. I enjoy working on teams, whether that’s collaborating on a UAV competition, guiding musicians as a section leader in a 300+ member marching band, or supporting customers in a fast-paced retail environment. Whatever I’m building, I’m constantly iterating, learning, and pushing myself to create experiences that feel precise, engaging, and unforgettable.
